About NYSCA-SL.

The National Youth Service Corps Alumni Sierra Leone (NYSCA-SL) was founded on the 1st of September, 2019 in adhearance to Chapter ten (10) sub section 10.4 of the National Youth Service Strategy and roadmap for the implementation which states that " just like universities, the NYS should establish an Alumni Association for those who have passed through the NYS.
The Alumni may be willing to donate substantial resources and help fund mobilization for the NYS".
